Web Development Workshop



The Railway Bus Management System is a real-time tracking system that aims to facilitate the railways’ passengers to transport them from distributed locations throughout the city/region to their respective railway stations conveniently and within time. Further this model shall reduce overcrowding at stations and passenger traffic on roads.

The system takes train's arrival information and passengers’ location as input. It analyses the information and provides the passengers an interactive list and routes of appropriate buses available near them with their appropriate timings which can drop them directly at their station within time.
An Admin module controls all the predefined factors like management of buses, routes, timings, conductors, logins; handling availability of buses etc.
